Match Columns

September 21, 2020

Match Columns When we decided to put our display up at Clackamas County 'Scare'grounds this October, we knew if we wanted to take the demons and the metal Cemetery sign from our columns, we would need to make columns to match the real ones on our driveway. We had to use a full wood frame to make this as heavy as possible because the metal frame that goes over the arch it has some weight and we did not want it to topple forward. There is a lip of wood at the top that is not foam covered, that is where the demons will slide down onto, similar to our current set up.
