Home Style Jerky

  • 1/4 teaspon of Prague Powder
  • 1/2 teaspon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon of garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon of liquid smoke mesquite
  • A dash of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 pound of thin sliced venison
  1. Begin by mixing all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Next add your sliced venison to the bowl and coat.
  3. Take meat out in pieces and set in dehydrator.
  4. Set your dehydrator to deer jerky.
  5. When your device alarm goes off your jerky is ready!
  6. ENJOY!

Home Style Jerky

  • 2 cups Dr. Pepper
  • 2 jalapenos (sliced)
  • 1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • A dash of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder

  2. Next add your sliced venison to the bowl and coat.
